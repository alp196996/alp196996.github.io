# 💾 Commands

## Player Commands

<table tabindex="0">
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Usage</th>
            <th>Description</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/addbounty &lt;player&gt; &lt;amount&gt;</code></td>
            <td>Creates a bounty/adds money to current bounty.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties</code></td>
            <td>Displays current bounties.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/hunterlb</code></td>
            <td>Displays the bounty hunter leaderboard.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties titles</code></td>
            <td>Displays owned titles.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties animations</code></td>
            <td>Displays owned bounty animations.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/redeembountyheads</code></td>
            <td>Redeem heads from your victims.</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>

## Admin Commands

<table tabindex="0">
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Usage</th>
            <th>Description</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties help</code></td>
            <td>Displays the help page.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties remove &lt;player&gt;</code></td>
            <td>Removes a bounty on a player.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties edit &lt;player&gt;</code></td>
            <td>Edit a bounty (edit reward &amp; contributors).</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><code>/bounties reload</code></td>
            <td>Reloads the plugin config files.</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
</table>

<p>When performing <code>/addbounty</code> as an admin, you may add <code>-bmm</code> at the end of your syntax to entirely bypass the minimum/maximum bounty reward limit. You may also use <code>-nt</code> to entirely negate the bounty creation tax.</p>
